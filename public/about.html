About FitTrack

FitTrack is a lightweight, intuitive workout tracking web app built during a 36-hour hackathon. It helps users log their workouts, track progress, and stay motivated on their fitness journey.

Our goal was to create a simple and accessible tool for anyone — whether you're a beginner or a gym veteran — to track your workouts without distractions or unnecessary complexity.

The app is built using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Firebase for real-time authentication and data storage.

This project was developed by two passionate students who love tech and lifting — and wanted to combine the two.

Thank you for checking it out!
